# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Design-conscious candle and fragrance shoppers — people who appreciate intentional living, slow rituals, and botanical clarity. They browse on mobile and desktop, discover new scents visually, and decide to purchase on Etsy. Their context is self-care: evening browsing, gift hunting, or reframing a space with intentional scent.

## Product Purpose

Candera is a digital storefront and micro-batch artisan platform for hand-poured botanical candles in numbered batches. It merges traditional craft with a modern web experience — an invitation to slow down, buy intentionally, and connect with the ritual of scent. Success means the visitor leaves feeling they've discovered something made with care, not just another product.

## Brand Personality

**Warm, editorial, intentional.** Grounded but not rustic. Refined but not cold. Poetic without being precious. The voice of a knowledgeable botanist who also happens to be a designer — precise about materials, generous with space, confident in typography.

References: Otherland, Boy Smells, P.F. Candle Co. — the editorial confidence of their visual language, not their specific design patterns.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic mass-market candle aesthetics: overly rustic/farmhouse visual language, Mason jar tropes, stock-photo candle imagery, the "scent marketing" cliché of generic botanicals. Candera is craft, not commodity. Every visual choice should read as intentional, not category-default.

## Design Principles

1. **Practice what you preach** — the site embodies the stillness and intentionality Candera sells. Every animation, transition, and layout choice should feel as considered as a numbered batch pour.
2. **Botanical clarity** — every element communicates scent, material, and craft without ambiguity. Copy is precise; visuals are specific; no qualitative words posing as quantitative facts.
3. **Editorial confidence** — generous whitespace, air, and typography as the primary design material. The page breathes. Nothing fights for attention.
4. **Thorough craft** — every state (empty, loading, error, success, focus, disabled) is handled with the same care as a numbered candle label. No surface ships unfinished.
5. **Honest presentation** — labels match actions. No inflated copy. No "ritual transition" when you mean "open Etsy." The brand voice earns trust by being precise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA target. Visible `:focus-visible` ring system on all interactive elements. Keyboard navigable throughout. Reduced motion support with `prefers-reduced-motion` media queries. Minimum 44px touch targets per Fitts's Law. No information conveyed by color alone.
