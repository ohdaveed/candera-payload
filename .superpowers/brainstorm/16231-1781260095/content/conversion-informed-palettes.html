Conversion-informed palette recommendations

Research synthesis: high contrast matters more than raw brightness; CTAs should be a restrained 10% accent; luxury ecommerce commonly uses black, white/cream, gold, deep purple, and pink; too many colors increases cognitive load.

60/30/10 rule: dominant neutral, secondary brand color, small high-contrast CTA accent.
Luxury signal: black, white/cream, gold, and deep purple communicate exclusivity.
CTA signal: warm accents like coral, red, amber, or vivid pink create action/urgency when used sparingly.
Accessibility: high contrast and simple 3–5 color palettes should be enforced per theme.
