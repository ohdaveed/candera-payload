The Story

Scarlet Bloom embodies romantic, slightly moody beauty with quiet magnetism. Like stepping into a candlelit room lined with warm wood and filled with soft conversation, this candle captures the essence of wild and refined femininity.

A miniature botanical garden preserved in wax, featuring real dried crimson roses, airy white blossoms, and eucalyptus leaves. The crisp botanical brightness opens with fresh greenery and juniper, while the warm, grounding base of aged woods creates an atmosphere of cozy elegance and deeply personal warmth.
