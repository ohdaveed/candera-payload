Our Story

"Self-possessed & effortlessly inviting, each candle embodies quiet luxury and soft femininity."

Born from a Simple
Belief

CANDERA was founded on the belief that the objects we surround ourselves with should be as intentional as the moments we create with them. In a world of mass production and fleeting trends, we chose a different path.

Each candle is designed to burn for exactly 50 hours—not by accident, but by design. Fifty hours is enough time to mark a season, celebrate a milestone, or simply create a ritual of presence in your daily life.

Crafted with
Purpose

Our candles are hand-poured in small batches using only the finest natural wax and carefully curated fragrance oils. Each vessel is selected for its ability to live on long after the last flame is extinguished.

We work with artisans who share our commitment to quality and sustainability, ensuring that every step of our process honors both the craft and the environment.

Our Values

The principles that guide every decision we make, from sourcing to shipping.

Sustainability

We use only natural, renewable materials and work with suppliers who share our commitment to environmental responsibility.

Intention

Every candle is designed to create moments of mindfulness and presence, transforming ordinary spaces into sanctuaries of calm.

Excellence

We never compromise on quality, from the purity of our wax to the precision of our burn time, ensuring every candle meets our exacting standards.
